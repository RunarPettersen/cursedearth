Recomended dark cities to visit:

London

London is full of dark gems for everybody intresting in history. From Jack the Ripper sightsing to the historical Tower of London.

Prague

Visit the dark past of Prague. A city full of old history with torture, ghosts, and a city under the city.

Budapest

Budapest is the grand capital city of Hungary, and has a dark past that you can visit. The city has experience everything from vampires and ghost to nazis and communists. The city has large medival castle to underground secrets.

Kraków

An old castle, and an even older dragon. Kraków is the city to visit if you like castles with dragons.

Salem

Salem, Massachusetts, is one of the most infamous dark history locations in the United States, primarily due to the Salem Witch Trials of 1692.

Vienna

Vienna has a lot to visit for any dark tourist.
